## Collaboration & Partnership Opportunities

OWASP Lahore welcomes collaboration with companies, universities, and tech communities.

### How to Collaborate

**For Companies:**
- Host OWASP meetups at your office
- Sponsor security awareness sessions
- Provide venue for workshops
- Support community events

**For Universities:**
- Host student-focused security workshops
- Invite OWASP speakers for guest lectures
- Partner for security research projects
- Support student mentorship programs

**For Tech Communities:**
- Co-organize security events
- Share knowledge and resources
- Cross-community collaboration
- Joint initiatives

### What We Offer

- **Security Awareness Sessions:** Educate your team about application security
- **Workshops:** Hands-on training on secure coding and OWASP tools
- **Expert Talks:** Industry professionals sharing real-world experiences
- **Networking:** Connect with Lahore's security community
